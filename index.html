About Us

This project was born out of a personal experience I had when struggling to find efficient ways to manage medical appointments and records for my family. Inspired by this challenge, I embarked on a journey to create Allomedcin, a platform that simplifies healthcare management for both patients and healthcare professionals. Throughout the development process, I encountered various obstacles but remained driven by my passion for improving healthcare accessibility. This project serves as a testament to my dedication to making a meaningful impact in the healthcare industry. This project is part of my Portfolio for Holberton School.
